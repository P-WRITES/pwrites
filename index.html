OUR BLUE HAIR FRIENDS

To be fair and just, on a good day they don't make soo much noise and on rare, very rare, situations they allow some sense in them and say some sensible things. In a nutshell, they are full of shit. Though they have soo many self created problems, it's a mystery how they have survived in this 'unfair,inconsiderate and patyriachal' world -as they call it... They are very traumatized that men built them the good world they live in. Why should any human being do such aterrible thing to them... "If they cannot handle us as we are,sorry that we are not sorry," said one blue hair colored friend,"we shaa get ourselves dogs and cats and age gracefully and happily!" The friend finished and walked away magestically with a better part of the pride and respect "they" command...
